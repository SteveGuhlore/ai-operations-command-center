# Revenue Pod Activation Checklist

## Goal

Define how to activate one revenue pod at a time in a controlled way.

## Activation rule

Only activate one pod at a time until the workflow is proven safe, reviewable, and cost-contained.

## Checklist

1. Validate the command center foundation.
2. Validate the pod config.
3. Confirm assigned agents and required tools are already defined.
4. Confirm approval gates are documented.
5. Start with `first_safe_tasks` only.
6. Keep publishing, spending, purchasing, and real account actions disabled.
7. Review output quality and cost before expanding scope.

## Early safe focus

Pods should begin with:

- briefs
- prompts
- copy drafts
- asset plans
- summaries
- reports

## Not yet allowed

- real publishing
- real purchases
- real account actions
- unsupervised pod expansion

## Stop conditions

- Stop if the pod requires external posting.
- Stop if the pod requires spending above approved limits.
- Stop if the pod requires credentials or integrations that are not reviewed.
- Stop if output quality is not consistently reviewable.
