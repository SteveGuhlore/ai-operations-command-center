You've got 100 prompts. Every single one is written, tested, and ready to go.

Here's how to get the most out of this pack:

**Step 1 — Copy any prompt exactly as written.**
Open ChatGPT (free or Plus), Claude, or Gemini and paste the prompt directly into the chat box.

**Step 2 — Run it first, then customise.**
Every prompt works as-is. Once you see the output, tell the AI: *"Redo this but make it about sustainable fashion"* or *"Make the tone more casual"* — one follow-up line is all it takes to make it yours.

# ✦ BONUS TIPS: Getting Better Outputs Every Time

**Tip 1 — Tell it who you are.**
Start any prompt with "I am a [fitness creator / travel blogger / finance YouTuber]" and your outputs will immediately feel more relevant and on-brand.

**Tip 2 — Tell it your audience.**
Add "My audience is [25–35 year old women who are new to investing]" and the AI will automatically adjust tone, vocabulary, and examples.

**Tip 3 — Ask for options, not just one answer.**
End prompts with "Give me 5 versions" or "Give me 3 different tones." More options = better chances of finding the one that clicks.

**Tip 4 — Use the word "rewrite."**
If the output is close but not quite right, say "Rewrite this but make it funnier / shorter / more direct." It's faster than starting over.

**Tip 5 — Chain your prompts.**
Use the output from one prompt as the input for the next. Example: get 10 video ideas (Prompt 1), pick the best one, then run Prompt 22 to get a full outline for it. This is where the real power is.

**Tip 6 — Save what works.**
Paste your best prompts + their outputs into a private Google Doc or Notion page. After 30 days, you'll have a personalised prompt library built around your specific niche and voice.

**Tip 7 — Add "in my voice."**
Paste a paragraph you've already written and say "Here's an example of how I write: [paste it]. Now rewrite your last response in this voice." This is the fastest way to make AI content sound like you.
