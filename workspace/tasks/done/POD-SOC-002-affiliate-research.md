# TikTok Shop Affiliate Research — Top 10 Products to Promote

## Goal
Research and identify 10 TikTok Shop products that are:
1. Currently trending or high-converting
2. Relevant to our audience (creators, freelancers, productivity-focused people, general consumers)
3. Commission rate 10%+ where possible
4. Priced $10–$80 (sweet spot for impulse buys)
5. Easy to make a 30-second demo/lifestyle video for

## Research Method
Use web_research to find:
- TikTok Shop trending products 2025
- High-commission TikTok affiliate products
- "TikTok made me buy it" categories that are still performing
- Products that work well in "lifestyle" or "day in my life" content

## Output Format

### TikTok Shop Affiliate Product Report

For each product (10 total):

**Product: [name]**
- Category: ...
- Estimated price: $...
- Why it's trending: ...
- Video angle: [exactly how to frame the 30-second video]
- Hook: [the exact first line of the promo script]
