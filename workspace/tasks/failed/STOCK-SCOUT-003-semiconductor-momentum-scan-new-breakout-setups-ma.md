Tony wants to know if there are new valid breakout setups forming in semis that his scanner may be about to flag.

Research and identify 3–5 semiconductor tickers showing credible breakout structure heading into the week of May 26, 2026. This is NOT a buy list — it's a watchlist research brief to prime Tony's scanner awareness.

## Deliverables
Save to `workspace/content/research/semi-momentum-scan-2026-05-25.md`

5. **Risk Flag** — What would invalidate the setup?

- Any sector-wide headwinds (export restrictions, supply chain news, earnings season risk)?

### Screening Criteria Used
Document the criteria you applied to narrow your list (e.g., "trading within 5% of 52-week high," "3-week base," "average volume > 1M").
