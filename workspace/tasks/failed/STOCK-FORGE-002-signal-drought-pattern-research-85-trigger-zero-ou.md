# Signal Drought Pattern Research — Terminal Outcome Drought Analysis

## Context
Tony Stocks' scanner has recorded an unusual event: **85 triggered setups with 0 terminal outcomes over a 4-day window** (as of 2026-05-22). This is described internally as a "terminal outcome drought." In normal cycles, triggered setups resolve (hit target, stop, or time-out) within a predictable window. A 4-day, 85-trigger drought with zero resolutions is statistically notable.

Tony needs to understand:
- Whether this pattern has historical precedent in the broader market
- What market regimes tend to produce "breakout limbo" — where setups trigger but don't resolve
- What typically happens AFTER a drought ends (flush of resolutions, or continued stall?)

## Goal
Produce a historical and analytical research brief on "breakout resolution drought" patterns in equity markets.

## Deliverables
Save to `workspace/content/research/signal-drought-pattern-2026-05-25.md`

### Section 1 — What Causes Breakout Resolution Stalls?
Research and explain the 3–5 most common market conditions that cause triggered technical breakouts to stall without resolving:
- Low volatility / VIX compression
- Pre-event uncertainty (upcoming FOMC, CPI, earnings)
- Sector rotation in progress (money moving between themes)
- Liquidity withdrawal (holidays, low volume weeks)
- Distribution masquerading as consolidation

For each: explain the mechanism and how to detect which one is active.

### Section 2 — Historical "Limbo" Periods in US Equities
Research 2–3 historical periods where the broad market saw an unusual number of stalled breakouts or range-bound conditions:
- What was the macro backdrop?
- How long did the stall last?
- How did it resolve (breakout flush upward, breakdown, choppy continuation)?

Suggested periods to research: Late 2015, Summer 2018, Aug–Sep 2023, early 2024.

### Section 3 — Post-Drought Resolution Patterns
- When a batch of stalled setups finally resolves, does the literature / historical data suggest they tend to resolve in ONE DIRECTION (momentum flush) or scatter (mixed outcomes)?
- Is there a "coiled spring" effect after low-resolution periods?
- What's the typical lag between stall period onset and resolution flush?

### Section 4 — Current Market Regime Assessment
Based on what you can research about current market conditions (week of 2026-05-26):
- Which of the causes from Section 1 best explain Tony's current drought?
- Is the drought likely near its end, or could it extend further?

### Section 5 — Forge's 3-Bullet Summary for Tony
- Most likely cause of current drought
- Expected resolution pattern (direction bias if any, or scatter)
- One leading indicator Tony should watch to signal drought is ending
