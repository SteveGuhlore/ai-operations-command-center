Each prompt in this pack is complete and ready to copy. Open ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, paste the prompt directly into the chat, and press send. You'll get a strong result immediately. ## Bonus Tips: Getting Even Better Results From AI

**Use specificity as your secret weapon.**
The more specific your prompt, the better the output. Instead of typing "write a YouTube intro," paste the actual video title, describe your audience in one sentence, and tell the AI the tone you want. You'll get something usable on the first try instead of the fifth.

**Chain your prompts.**
Use this pack in sequence. Generate your video idea with a Section 1 prompt, then immediately feed that idea into a Section 2 prompt to get an outline. Then use that outline with a Section 3 prompt to generate titles. One idea becomes a fully planned piece of content in under ten minutes.

**Save your best outputs.**
When AI gives you something that really works — a hook, a caption format, a newsletter tone — save it and use it as a reference. Paste it into future prompts and say "write something in the same style as this example." This trains the AI to match your voice.

**Iterate, don't restart.**
If an output is 80% right, don't start over — reply with a short follow-up like "make the opening more direct" or "shorten this to 100 words" or "give me a version that's less formal." Iteration is faster than regeneration.

**Make it sound like you.**
After AI gives you a draft, spend two minutes reading it aloud. Swap any phrase that doesn't sound like you. Change one word per sentence if needed. This is what separates creators who use AI effectively from those whose content sounds robotic.
